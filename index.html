About Us

Committed to your dental health.

A family-owned Dental Clinic

Established in 2021, we bring together the expertise of two passionate dentists specializing in Prosthodontics and Orthodontics. With a deep commitment to excellence, we focus on crafting Hollywood-worthy smiles through premium-quality dental care tailored to each patient’s unique needs.

At our clinic, we believe that a beautiful, healthy smile goes beyond aesthetics—it boosts confidence and enhances overall well-being. That’s why we combine advanced techniques with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring optimal results for every patient. Our work ethics and patient-centered approach has earned us the trust of a diverse clientele from Kosovo and the Diaspora, who seek world-class dental treatments in a welcoming and professional environment.

Whether you need restorative solutions, orthodontic care, or a complete smile transformation, our goal is to provide exceptional, long-lasting results with a personal touch. Your smile is our passion, and we are here to make it shine!

case 1 - Prosthodontics

Stunning Smile Makeover: Transforming Aged Dental Work into a Natural, Youthful Look with Zirconia Bridges

This image shows a dental before-and-after transformation of our patient - a lady in her 60ties:

(Before): Old PFM bridges that are discolored (yellowish) with visible staining Noticeable spacing and misalignment, particularly between the front teeth. Teeth have irregular shapes and uneven edges.

(After): Zirconia multilayer bridge that shows a significantly improved aesthetics with whiter and evenly colored teeth. Alignment and spacing have been corrected. Edges are smooth and the dental arch is in its anatomical harmony.

Case 2 - Prosthodontics

Complete Smile Transformation: From Gummy Smile to Glamorous with Expert Gum Contouring and Zirconia Crowns by OXADENT

This transformation reflects a combination of periodontal recontouring and cosmetic dentistry done by OXADENT for a stunning, confident smile.

Top image (Before): Teeth show significant discoloration and mild crowding. Uneven gum lines and excess gingival display (gummy smile). Teeth appear short due to excessive gum coverage.

Bottom image (After): Zirconia multilayer crowns result with a brighter, well-aligned, and evenly shaped teeth. Crown lengthening and gingivectomy were performed to sculpt the gum line, exposing more of the natural tooth and creating a balanced smile. The result is a longer-looking tooth appearance and enhanced overall aesthetics that represents a beautiful and catchy Hollywood Smile.

  • Category: Prosthodontics

Case 3 - Prosthodontics

Full-Mouth Restoration: Reviving Damaged Teeth with Durable and Natural-Looking Zirconia Crowns for a Healthy, Confident Smile

This transformation highlights a full smile rehabilitation using zirconia multilayer crowns:

Top image (Before): Severe discoloration, decay, and worn-down teeth. Irregular shapes and compromised enamel with visible plaque buildup.

Bottom image (After): A dramatic improvement with bright, natural-looking zirconia multilayer crowns. Restored function and esthetics with enhanced tooth shape, alignment, and uniform color. The multilayer zirconia provides both strength and lifelike translucency, ideal for a durable and aesthetic full-mouth restoration. A complete smile revival, giving a healthy, confident look thanks to advanced restorative dentistry.
